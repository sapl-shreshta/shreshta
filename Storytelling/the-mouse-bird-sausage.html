📖 The Tale

Once upon a time, a mouse, a bird, and a sausage became the best of friends. They lived together and shared their duties fairly. The mouse kept house and made the fire, the bird fetched wood from the forest, and the sausage cooked and seasoned the meals.

They were very happy with their arrangement until one day, another bird came to visit. This visitor suggested that each of them should trade jobs to see if they could do better. Eager to prove themselves, the three friends agreed to the change.

What happens when the mouse tries to fetch wood? What becomes of the bird trying to cook? And what danger awaits the sausage when it tries to keep the house? Find out in this timeless tale of friendship and the importance of staying true to our roles and responsibilities!
