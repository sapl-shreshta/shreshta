Garudagamana Tava Carana Kamalam

Devotional hymn to Lord Vishnu

📖 Lyrics - Garudagamana Tava Carana Kamalam

📍 VERSE 1
Garuḍagamana tava caraṇakamalamiha
Manasi lasatu mama nityam | manasi lasatu mama nityam ॥
Mama tāpamapākuru deva | mama pāpamapākuru deva ॥
Word-Word Meaning:
garuḍa-gamana = Oh (God), who travels on Garuda
tava = your
caraṇa-kamalam = lotus feet
lasatu = let (it) shine
nityam = always
iha = here
mama manasi = in my mind
deva = O God
apākuru = remove
mama = my
tāpam = sufferings
pāpam = sins

Meaning: O God who travels on Garuda, let your lotus feet always shine in my mind. O God, remove my sufferings and sins.
📍 VERSE 2
Jalajanayana vidhinamuciharaṇamukha
Vibudhavinutapadapadma | vibudhavinutapadapadma
Mama tāpamapākuru deva | mama pāpamapākuru deva ॥
Word-Word Meaning:
jalaja-nayana = O (God), whose eyes are like lotus
pada-padma = O (God), who has lotus feet
vinuta = that is worshipped by
vibudha = numerous learned ones
mukha = starting with
vidhi = Brahma (and)
namuci-haraṇa = Indra, the killer of Namuci

Meaning: O God whose eyes are like lotus flowers, whose lotus feet are worshipped by Brahma and other learned gods, remove my sufferings and sins.
📍 VERSE 3
Bhujagaśayana bhava madanajanaka mama
Jananamaraṇa-bhayahārī | jananamaraṇa-bhayahārī
Mama tāpamapākuru deva | mama pāpamapākuru deva ॥
Word-Word Meaning:
bhujaga-śayana = O (God), whose bed is the serpent
madana-janaka = O (God), the father of Kaama
bhava = Become
bhaya-hārī = dispeller of the fear
janana-maraṇa = of life and death

Meaning: O God who rests on the cosmic serpent, the father of desire, become the dispeller of the fear of birth and death. Remove my sufferings and sins.
📍 VERSE 4
Śaṅkhacakradhara duṣṭadaityahara
Sarvaloka-śaraṇa | sarvaloka-śaraṇa
Mama tāpamapākuru deva | mama pāpamapākuru deva ॥
Word-Word Meaning:
śaṅkha-cakra-dhara = O (God), who adorns the conch and the disc
duṣṭa-daitya-hara = O (God), the destroyer of the wicked Asuras
sarva-loka-śaraṇa = O (God), the sole refuge for the entire world

Meaning: O God who carries the sacred conch and disc, the destroyer of evil demons, the sole refuge for all worlds, remove my sufferings and sins.
📍 VERSE 5
Agaṇita-guṇagaṇa aśaraṇaśaraṇada
Vidalita-suraripujāla | vidalita-suraripujāla
Mama tāpamapākuru deva | mama pāpamapākuru deva ॥
Word-Word Meaning:
agaṇita-guṇa-gaṇa = O (God), who possesses a collection of innumerable good qualities
aśaraṇa-śaraṇada = O (God), who is the giver of protection to those without any refuge
vidalita-sura-ripu-jāla = O (God), who destroyed the collection of Asuras, the enemies of the Devas

Meaning: O God possessing innumerable qualities, the giver of refuge to the helpless, who destroyed the armies of demons, remove my sufferings and sins.
📍 VERSE 6
Bhaktavaryamiha bhūrikaruṇayā
Pāhi bhāratītīrtham | pāhi bhāratītīrtham
Mama tāpamapākuru deva | mama pāpamapākuru deva ॥
Word-Word Meaning:
pāhi = You protect
iha = here
bhūri-karuṇayā = with overwhelming compassion
bhāratītīrtham = Sri Bharati Tirtha Swami
bhakta-varyam = who is a principal devotee

Meaning: O God, with your overwhelming compassion, please protect here Sri Bharati Tirtha Swami, the principal devotee. Remove my sufferings and sins.
