Ragam: Hindolam (20 Natabhairavi Janya) | Taalam: Adi
Arohanam: S G2 M1 D1 N2 S | Avarohanam: S N2 D1 M1 G2 S
📍 PALLAVI
koṇḍalalō nelakonna kōnēṭi rāyaḍu vāḍu
koṇḍalanta varamulu guppeḍu vāḍu ॥ (2.5)
Word-Word Meaning: కొండలలో Kondalalo = in the Hills (Konda = hill)
నెలకొన్న Nelakonna = to become fixed/firm
కోనేటి Koneti = a square tank with steps into it
రాయడు Rayadu = Lord/King
వాడు Vadu = He
కొండలం Kondalantha = as big as the hill/mountain
వరములు Varamulu = boons
గుప్పెడు Guppedu = fistful/sprinkle
వాడు Vaadu = one (masculine)/He
Meaning: He is the Lord of the Koneti (the pond); who became fixed on the Hills. Boons as big as the mountains but (He is only fistful/) sprinkling one.
📍 CHARANAM 1
kummara dāsuḍaina kuruvarati nambi
immanna varamulella ichchinavāḍu । (2)
dommulu sēsina yaṭṭi toṇḍamān chakkuravarti (2)
rammanna chōṭiki vachchi nammina vāḍu ॥ (1)
Word-Word Meaning: కుమ్మర Kummara = Potter దాసుడైన Dasudaina = who is the slave కురువరతి నంబి Kuruvati Nambi = (name) ఇమ్మన్న Immanna = whatever asked for వరములెల్ల Varamulella = all the boons నిచ్చిన Nichina = who gave వాడు Vaadu = He దొమ్ములు Dommulu = squabble సేసిన Chesina = has done యట్టి Yatti = that/who తొండమాం Tondaman = చక్కురవర్తి Chakravarthi = King రమ్మన్న Rammanna = wherever asked to come చోటికి Chotiki = place వచ్చి Vacchi = by coming నమ్మిన Nammina = believed/trusted వాడు Vaadu = He Meaning: He is the one who gave The potter slave/servant Kuruvati Nambi all the boons he asked for. He is the one who trusted The Tondaman, the King, who always fought wars, by coming wherever he asked Him to come.
📍 CHARANAM 2
achchapu vēḍukatōḍa nanantāḻvāruki
muchchili veṭṭiki mannu mōsinavāḍu । (2)
machchika dolaka tirunambi tōḍuta (2)
nichcha nichcha māṭalāḍi nochchinavāḍu ॥ (1)
Word-Word Meaning: అచ్చప Acchapu = with pure/transparent
వేడుకతోడ Vedukathodanu = with grandeur
అనంతాళువారికి Ananthaluvariki = to Anantha Alvar
ముచ్చిలి Mucchili = back of the neck
వెట్టికి Vettiki = for unpaid labor
మన్ను Mannu = mud
మోచిన Mochina = who bore/carried
వాడు Vaadu = He
మచ్చిక Macchika = love
తోలక Tolaka = without hiding/driving away
దిరుమలనంబి Thirimala Nambi = (name)
తోడుత Thodutha = immediately, forthrightly
నిచ్చ నిచ్చ Nityaa Nityaa = day after day/everyday
మాటలాడి Mataladi = by speaking
నొచ్చిన Nochina = perform a religious act
వాడు Vaadu = He
Meaning: With pure grandeur for Annantha Alvar, He carried mud on the back of his neck with unpaid labor. Without hiding his love for Thirumala Nambi, He is the religious one, who fortrightly spoke day after day!
📍 CHARANAM 3
kañchilōna nuṇḍa dirukachchinambi mīda
karuṇiñchi tana yeḍaku rappiñchina vāḍu । (2)
yeñchi ekkuḍaina vēṅkaṭēśuḍu manalaku (2)
mañchivāḍai karuṇa bāliñchina vāḍu ॥ (1)
Word-Word Meaning: కంచిలో Kanchilo = In Kanchi
నుండ Nunda = who was there
తిరుకచ్చినంబి Thirukachi Nambi = (name)
మీద Meeda = on కరుణించి Karuninchi = by having compassion
తన Thana = himself
యెడకు Yedaku = in front of
రప్పించిన Rappinchi = made to come
వాడు Vaadu = He
ఎంచి Yenchi = by measuring/discretion
యెక్కుడైన Yekkadaina = whereever
వేంకటేశుడు Venkateshudu (Venkata + eeshudu) = Venkata's Deity
మనలకు Manalaku = to us
మంచివాడై Manchivadai = by becoming good person
కరుణ Karuna = mercy
పాలించిన Paalinchina = to rule
వాడు Vaadu = He
Meaning: He is the one who made Thirukachi Nambi who was in Kanchi come to Him out of compassion. By discretion, anytime Venkateshudu is the one to guard us as He is the good person with mercy.
