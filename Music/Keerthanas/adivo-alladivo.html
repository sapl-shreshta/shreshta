Adivo Alladivo

Sri Venkateswara keerthana - Ragam Madhyamavati

Ragam: Madhyamavati (22 Kharaharapriya Janya)

Arohanam: S R2 M1 P D1 N2 S

Avarohanam: S N2 P M1 R2 S

Talum: Adi

📖 Lyrics

📍 PALLAVI (Main Theme)
adivō alladivō śrī hari vāsamu
padivēla śēṣula paḍagala mayamu ॥ (2.5)
Meaning: అదివో adivo = that
అల్లదివో alladivo = that far away (over there)
శ్రీ హరి sri hari = venkatesa's
వాసము vaasamu = house
పది వేల padi vela = ten thousand
శేషుల sesula = snakes
పడగల padagala = hoods
మయము mayamu = filled with
That is the holy place where Adisesha appears to be covered with his ten thousand hoods is Sri Maha Vishnu's abode.

అఖిల akhila = most

అదివో adivo = that (over there)
బ్రహ్మాదులకు brahmadulaku = for lord brahma and others

అఖిల akhila = most
మునులకు munulaku = for saints
అదే ade = that is
చూడుడు choodudu = see it
అదే ade = that is
మొక్కుడు = pray to it
ఆనందమయము anandamayamu = which brings a lot of happiness
That is Venkatachalam, which is superior to everything else in the universe. It is the most vital to "Brahma" (the embodiment of God, the Lord of creation) and others. That is the permanent abode of all saints. See that great Venkatachalam, bow down to it and attain bliss.

That is the "sheshaachalamu" (one of the seven hills of Tirumala where Lord Venkateswara resides), which is near to us! It is the true abode of the celestial deities.
