🔁 Circle Angles

Circles have special angle relationships that are important in geometry and engineering:

Central Angle

A central angle has its vertex at the center of the circle and its sides extend to the circle.

  • The angle measure equals the arc measure
  • A full circle = 360°

Inscribed Angle

An inscribed angle has its vertex on the circle and its sides are chords of the circle.

⭐ Rule: Inscribed angle = ½ × Central angle (same arc)

Special Circle Angle Rules

  • Angle in a semicircle: Any angle inscribed in a semicircle = 90° (right angle!)
  • Angles in the same segment: All inscribed angles subtending the same arc are equal
  • Tangent-radius angle: A tangent line to a circle is always perpendicular (90°) to the radius at that point

🏗️ Architecture & Construction

Architects and builders use angles every day to design safe and beautiful structures:

🏠 Roof Design

Roof angles determine water drainage and snow load capacity. Common angles: 30°-45°

🪟 Windows & Doors

Right angles (90°) are essential for proper fit and functionality

🌉 Bridges

Support angles must be precise to distribute weight safely

⛪ Arches

Ancient and modern arches use specific angles for strength

🧭 Navigation & Direction

Angles are critical for finding direction and location:

🧭 Compass Bearings

Angles from North (0°) to measure direction: East 90°, South 180°, West 270°

✈️ Flight Paths

Pilots use angles to navigate and maintain course

🗺️ Map Reading

Angles help determine distance and direction between locations

🛰️ Satellite Navigation

GPS uses precise angle calculations for positioning

⚙️ Engineering & Mechanics

Engineers use angles to make machines work correctly:

🔧 Gears

Gears mesh at specific angles to transfer power efficiently

🚗 Vehicle Design

Wheel alignment angles affect handling and tire wear

📡 Antenna Positioning

Antennas must point at specific angles to receive signals

⚡ Solar Panels

Panels positioned at optimal angles capture maximum sunlight
