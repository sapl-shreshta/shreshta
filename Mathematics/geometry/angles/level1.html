💡 What is an Angle?

An angle is formed when two lines or rays meet at a single point. That point is called the vertex.

Think of it like the opening between two sticks held at one end. The wider the opening, the bigger the angle!

🌍 Where Do We See Angles in Real Life?

Angles are everywhere around you! You just need to look for them:

  • 🏠 Corners of rooms: The corner where two walls meet forms a 90° angle
  • ⏰ Clock hands: The hands of a clock form different angles at different times
  • 🍕 Pizza slice: The pointy part is an angle - bigger slices have bigger angles!
  • 📐 Roof of a house: The slanted roof creates angles
  • ✂️ Open scissors: The more you open scissors, the bigger the angle
  • 📖 Open book: The angle between the pages gets bigger as you open it
  • 🎯 Arrow or cone: The point is formed by angles
  • 📐 Slides and ramps: They form angles with the ground

🔄 Understanding Angles Through Turning

Another way to think about angles is through turning or rotation:

  • Quarter turn: Spinning 1/4 of the way around = 90°
  • Half turn: Spinning halfway around = 180°
  • Full turn: Spinning all the way around = 360°
