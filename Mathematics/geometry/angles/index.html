Understanding Angles!

Master the amazing world of angles! 🌟

📐 What is an Angle?

An angle is formed when two lines meet at a point. That point is called the vertex.

Angles are everywhere around you:

  • 🏠 The corners of your room
  • 📐 The corner of your notebook
  • ⏰ The hands of a clock
  • 🍕 A slice of pizza
  • ✂️ Open scissors

📏 Types of Angles

Angles are measured in degrees (°). Different angles have different names:

  • Right Angle: Exactly 90° (like a corner of a square) ⊞
  • Acute Angle: Less than 90° (sharp and pointy)
  • Obtuse Angle: Between 90° and 180° (wide and open)
  • Straight Angle: Exactly 180° (forms a straight line)
  • Reflex Angle: Between 180° and 360° (extra wide!)

🎯 How to Measure Angles?

We use a tool called a protractor to measure angles!

Steps to measure an angle:

  • Place the center of the protractor at the vertex (the point where lines meet)
  • Align one line of the angle with the zero line of the protractor
  • Read the number where the other line crosses the protractor
  • That's your angle in degrees! ✨

💡 Fun Facts about Angles!

  • A full rotation around a point is 360 degrees! 🔄
  • The angles in a triangle always add up to 180 degrees! 📐
  • A circle has angles that go from 0° to 360°! 🔵
  • Protractors have been used for hundreds of years to measure angles! 📜
