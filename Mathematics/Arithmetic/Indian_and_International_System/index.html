Indian & International Systems

Master number naming systems! 📚

Different countries use different systems to name and write large numbers. In India, we use the Indian System with "lakhs" and "crores," while English-speaking countries use the International System with "millions" and "billions." Understanding both systems helps you work with numbers globally. In this chapter, you'll learn to convert between systems, place commas correctly, and write numbers in different forms.

📖 What You'll Learn:

  • Understanding the Indian numbering system (ones, tens, hundreds, thousands, lakhs, crores)
  • Understanding the International numbering system (ones, tens, hundreds, thousands, millions, billions)
  • Converting numbers between Indian and International systems
  • Placing commas correctly in both systems
  • Writing numbers in words and expanded form
  • Working with number names in different languages/systems
